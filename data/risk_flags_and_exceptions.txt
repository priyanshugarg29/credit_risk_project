Title: Risk Indicators and Exception Handling
Effective Date: 1 June 2025

High-risk flags include:
- Irregular income (e.g., seasonal, gig work)
- Multiple credit inquiries within 3 months
- Debt-to-income ratio > 50%
- History of late payments or defaults

Policy Exceptions:
- Applicants with low score (580–619) may be considered if:
   - Monthly income > £2,000 and
   - Debt-to-income ratio < 35%, or
   - A guarantor is available

All exceptions must be documented with justification and approved by Tier-2 or above.
