Title: Credit Risk Approval Matrix
Effective Date: 1 June 2025

Approval thresholds for personal loan applications:

- Score ≥ 620, Loan ≤ £10,000 → Auto-approval (system decision)
- Score 580–619 or Loan > £10,000 → Tier-2 Underwriter review
- Score < 580 or Loan > £25,000 → Tier-3 Senior Credit Officer approval
- Any exception to income requirements or irregular profile → Escalation to Credit Committee

All approvals must be logged with rationale, evidence, and decision authority.
