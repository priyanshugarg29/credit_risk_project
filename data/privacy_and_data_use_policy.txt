Title: Data Privacy and Consent Policy
Effective Date: 1 June 2025

Data collected for creditworthiness assessments is processed under Article 6(1)(b) of the UK GDPR.

- Open Banking data may be accessed only with explicit customer consent.
- All collected data must be stored securely and retained no longer than 12 months post-application, unless required for audit or legal purposes.
- Customers may revoke consent at any time, triggering deletion of associated financial data unless legal obligations apply.
